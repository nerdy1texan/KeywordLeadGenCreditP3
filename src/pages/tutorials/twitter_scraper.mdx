## How to Use the Twitter Web Scraper

1. **Log in to LeadNimbus**: Ensure you are logged in to your LeadNimbus account.
2. **Navigate to Twitter Scraper**: From your dashboard, go to the **Twitter Web Scraper** section.
3. **Enter the Twitter URL or Hashtag**: Paste the URL of the public Twitter profile or enter the hashtag you want to scrape.
4. **Start the Scraper**: Click on the "Scrape" button, and LeadNimbus will begin fetching data such as tweets, replies, follower information, and more.

## Data Collected

The Twitter Web Scraper will collect the following public data:
- **Profile Information**: Username, display name, bio, and profile image
- **Tweets and Replies**: Scrape public tweets and related replies
- **Follower Metrics**: Number of followers, following, and engagement rates
- **Hashtag Data**: For hashtag scrapes, collect tweets, users, and engagement metrics

## Exporting Data

Once the scraping is completed, you can export the data in **CSV format**. Simply click the **Download CSV** button, and the data will be ready for use in your CRM or marketing tool.
